Subscribe to Berkeley Fluids Mailing List

You can subscribe to Berkeley fluids mailing List by joining our lists using http://bgroups.berkeley.edu home page. Our list names are
  • Students and postdocs: fluids_students_and_postdocs
  • Faculty: fluids_faculty
Or you can send a subscription request to
  • Students and postdocs: fluids_students_and_postdocs+subscribe@berkeley.edu
  • Faculty: fluids_faculty+subscribe@berkeley.edu


Unsubscribe from Berkeley Fluids Mailing List

You can unsubscribe from Berkeley fluids mailing List by following these insctructions on the http://bgroups.berkeley.edu home page.
